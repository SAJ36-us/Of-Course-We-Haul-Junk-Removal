About Us

The founder and coordinator Seth Johnson is a local student at the university of South Carolina studying Information Integrated Technologies, but has a background in home renovations. From a young age he was very involved in the construction scene and part of that was to haul anything and everything away in order to clean up the properties. with this he now takes this to the residents who find themselves in need of a hand.

Seth and his team have experience in residential demolition and clean up ranging from the construction scene to the regular movers scene including safe piano transportation as a former piano deliveryman.

To display one message to the potential client our company motto is 'Of course we will haul it!' This is to let the local community know that no matter your messy hunky junk, we will solve it
